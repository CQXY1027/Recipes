Air Fryer Greek Chicken

Ingredients

  • 5-6 chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive Oil

    • Instructions

    1. Mix everything in a bowl
    2. Let the chicken marinate for 4-5h or overnight
    3. Air fry for 15 mins at 200°C
