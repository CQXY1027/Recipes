Air Fryer Salmon

Ingredients

  • 900g Salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Thyme
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 0.5 tsp Black pepper
  • 0.25 Lemon juice

    • Instructions

    1. Mix everything in a bowl
    2. Air fry for 7-8 mins
